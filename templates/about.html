This is the stuff about our project

Strange Sweets is a webstore built with Python, Django, and jQuery, and deployed via Heroku. It was developed by Keith Brandtjen, Britt Johnson and Rachael Wisecarver. To build this app we used the tools and processes learned throughout 301 and applied them to python and django. At time of deployment, we have most of the features of a full-scale eCommerce site. In the future we would like to add the checkout functionality in order to actually sell our products.
